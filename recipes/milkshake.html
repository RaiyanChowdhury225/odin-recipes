time for milkshake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup (optional)
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (for garnish, optional)

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you like a chocolate flavor, add chocolate syrup and blend again until mixed.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  4. Top with whipped cream and garnish with a maraschino cherry, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
